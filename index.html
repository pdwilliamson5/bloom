Things to Know

How to prepare for for a wax.
  • The hair must be grown out at least 1/4-1/2 an inch from last shave,wax or depilatory cream use, this is generally 2-3 weeks of growth.
  • Gently exfoliate a few days before scheduled service.
  • Avoid caffeine day of service if possible, this will help avoid an increase of skin sensitivity.
  • Take ibuprofen 30-45 min prior service to help decrease skin sensitivity and prevent inflammation.
  • Avoid any Acne or Anti-Aging creams, cleansers and exfoliates containing Retinoids,Glycolic,Salacylic, or Lactic acids for at least a week prior to service.
  • Clients who have used Accutane within the past 6 months are not advised to get waxed.
  • It will likely take 2-3 consecutive waxes to get all of the hair on the same growth cycle.
  • Consistency is key for best waxing results.
What to expect.
  • Consultation before service along with a new client form to fill out.
  • Service times generally take 30-45 min, of course these times may vary with each individual.
  • Some discomfort is to be expected with waxing as we are pulling the hair up from the root, keep in mind we will stay within YOUR comfort zone.
  • Clients may experience some skin irritation and or tenderness for generally 24-48 hrs after service.
  • Plan to take it easy on your skin for AT LEAST 24 hrs after waxing, try to avoid tanning and swimming of any kind, excessive sweating, heavy creams and or scented lotions.
  • Plan to return for follow up wax within 4-6 weeks.
  • Feel free to call,text or email with ANY question.
